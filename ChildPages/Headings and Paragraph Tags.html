Heading & Paragraph Tags


Summary of Header & Paragraph Tags








There are 6 heading tags, H1-H6.

Each heading tag ranges in size and h2 is the biggest unless it's used in the header.

Heading tags decrease in size the higher the number goes as long as it isn't higher than 6.

Heading tags also make the text bold.

The paragraph tag indicates a new paragraph by adding a space before and after the paragraph.

The strong tag makes the text inside it bold.

The EM tag turns the text inside it into the italic font.

The br tag is a self closing tag which essentially presses enter to take you to a new line.
